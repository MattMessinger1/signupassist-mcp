# SHIPWORX LLC REFUND POLICY
## SignupAssist Success Fee

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 15, 2026

---

## Overview

SignupAssist charges a **$20.00 Success Fee** only when a registration is successfully completed with the activity provider. This document explains when refunds are issued and how to request one.

---

## Understanding Our Fees

### What You Pay

| Fee Type | Amount | When Charged |
|----------|--------|--------------|
| **SignupAssist Autopilot Subscription** | $9.00/month | Monthly, while renewal is active |
| **SignupAssist Success Fee** | $20.00 | Only after successful registration |
| **Program Fee** | Varies | Paid directly to activity provider |

**Important:** The Success Fee is charged by SignupAssist (Shipworx LLC). The Program Fee is charged by and paid to the activity provider (e.g., the ski club, camp, or class provider).

### Subscription Cancellation

The SignupAssist Autopilot subscription can be canceled from the dashboard without emailing support. Canceling stops the next renewal, keeps access active through the paid period, and does not delete profiles or registration history.

Cancellation confirmation states: "You won't be charged again. Access continues until [date]."

---

## When Refunds ARE Issued

We will refund the $20.00 Success Fee in the following situations:

### 1. Provider Cancels the Registration
If the activity provider cancels your registration (e.g., class cancelled, program discontinued), we will automatically refund your Success Fee within 5-7 business days.

### 2. Technical Failure on Our End
If SignupAssist experiences a technical error that causes:
- Duplicate registrations
- Incorrect registration details that cannot be corrected
- Failed registration that was incorrectly marked as successful

### 3. Duplicate Charges
If you are charged the Success Fee more than once for the same registration, we will refund the duplicate charge(s).

### 4. Scheduled Registration Not Executed
If you scheduled a registration and it was not executed at the specified time due to a SignupAssist system failure (not due to sold-out availability or provider issues).

---

## When Refunds Are NOT Issued

We cannot refund the Success Fee in these situations:

### 1. Change of Mind
If you decide you no longer want the registration after it has been successfully completed.

### 2. Participant Cannot Attend
If the participant cannot attend due to illness, schedule conflicts, or other personal reasons after the registration is complete.

### 3. Provider's Cancellation Policy
Refunds for the **Program Fee** are subject to the activity provider's own cancellation and refund policies. SignupAssist has no control over provider refund policies.

### 4. Sold-Out Programs
If a scheduled registration cannot be completed because the program sold out before the signup window opened or immediately after.

### 5. Provider Account Issues
If the registration fails due to issues with your account at the activity provider (e.g., unpaid balance, suspended account, incomplete profile).

---

## How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Gather Your Information
Have ready:
- Your receipt number (starts with `REG-` or `SCH-`)
- The date of the registration
- A brief description of why you're requesting a refund

### Step 2: Contact Us
Email **support@shipworx.ai** with:
- Subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Receipt Number]"
- Your receipt number
- Reason for refund request

### Step 3: Response Time
- We will acknowledge your request within **2 business days**
- Refund decisions are typically made within **5 business days**
- Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days**

---

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:
- Credit/debit card refunds: 5-7 business days to appear on your statement
- The refund will appear as "SHIPWORX" or "SIGNUPASSIST" on your statement

---

## Provider Fee Refunds

**SignupAssist does not handle refunds for Program Fees.**

To request a refund for the program fee you paid to the activity provider:
1. Contact the activity provider directly
2. Follow their cancellation/refund policy
3. SignupAssist cannot intervene in provider refund decisions

---

## Disputes

If you believe a refund was incorrectly denied:
1. Reply to our refund decision email with additional information
2. We will review your case within 5 business days
3. Our decision on the second review is final

For unresolved disputes, please refer to our [Terms of Use](./TERMS_OF_USE.md) for the dispute resolution process.
